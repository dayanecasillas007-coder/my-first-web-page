About Me

Design is how I express creativity, emotion, and personal identity.

Who I Am

I am a creative person who enjoys looking at spaces, colors, shapes, and the way a room can make someone feel. I like design because it combines imagination with real-life needs. A good space is not only pretty; it should also be useful, comfortable, and meaningful.

My Design Interests

I enjoy modern interiors, cozy rooms, natural colors, artistic details, and organized layouts. I also like observing architecture and thinking about how different structures can change people’s daily lives.

My Goals

My goal is to keep learning about design and improve my ability to create spaces that help people feel calm, inspired, and at home. This website represents my first step in showing my ideas online.
